Inspector Steel

Inspector Steel is a cybernetic law enforcement hero from Raj Comics. After a life-threatening accident, his body was rebuilt using advanced mechanical technology, making him stronger, faster, and nearly indestructible. He protects the city using intelligence, technology, and powerful cybernetic abilities.

Powers & Abilities

  • Superhuman strength and durability
  • Bulletproof metallic body
  • Advanced sensors and targeting system
  • Built-in combat weapons
  • High-speed data processing and analysis
