Inspector Steel

Inspector Steel is a cybernetic law enforcer from Raj Comics. After a near-fatal incident, his body was rebuilt with advanced mechanical parts, giving him superhuman strength, durability, and powerful technological abilities to fight crime.

Powers & Abilities

  • Cybernetic super strength
  • Bulletproof steel body
  • Advanced targeting and scanning systems
  • Built-in weapons and gadgets
  • High-speed processing and analysis
