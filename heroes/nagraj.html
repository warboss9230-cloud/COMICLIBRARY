Nagraj

Nagraj is the king of snakes and one of the most powerful superheroes from Raj Comics. Created by the mysterious Naga civilization, he fights evil using his deadly snake powers, poison abilities, and superhuman strength.

Powers & Abilities

  • Control over millions of snakes
  • Deadly poison attacks
  • Superhuman strength and healing
  • Can transform body into snake swarm
  • Hypnosis and mind control
