Shakti

Shakti is a powerful female superhero blessed with divine energy. She possesses immense strength, energy projection abilities, and the courage to fight powerful enemies. Shakti represents power, determination, and justice.

Powers & Abilities

  • Superhuman strength and endurance
  • Energy blast and power projection
  • Flight ability
  • Enhanced reflexes and combat skills
  • Divine energy transformation
