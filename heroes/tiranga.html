Tiranga

Tiranga is a patriotic superhero dedicated to protecting the nation. With advanced combat skills, intelligence, and unwavering courage, he fights terrorism, crime, and threats to national security. He symbolizes bravery, discipline, and love for the country.

Skills & Abilities

  • Expert martial artist and combat specialist
  • Master of weapons and tactical operations
  • Highly trained intelligence officer
  • Peak physical strength and endurance
  • Advanced surveillance and strategy skills
