Bankelal

Bankelal is a humorous and clever character from Raj Comics. Known for his selfish yet funny plans to become king, his schemes always backfire and end up helping others. His stories combine comedy, magic, and unexpected heroism.

Traits & Abilities

  • Extremely clever and manipulative
  • Master of funny schemes and tricks
  • Strong survival instincts
  • Unintentional heroism
  • Often connected with magical situations
