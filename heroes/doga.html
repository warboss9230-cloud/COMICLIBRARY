Doga

Doga is the dark vigilante of Mumbai. Unlike traditional superheroes, he operates in the shadows and fights crime using brutal strength, weapons, and fearless determination. His mission is to eliminate evil and protect the innocent at any cost.

Skills & Abilities

  • Peak human strength and endurance
  • Expert hand-to-hand combat
  • Master of firearms and weapons
  • Fearless crime fighter
  • High pain tolerance and survival skills
