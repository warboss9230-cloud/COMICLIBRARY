Bhediya

Bhediya is a powerful werewolf hero from Raj Comics. Protector of the forests, he possesses the strength, speed, and instincts of a wild predator. He fights evil forces that threaten nature and humanity.

Powers & Abilities

  • Superhuman strength and speed
  • Enhanced senses and night vision
  • Rapid healing ability
  • Can transform into a powerful werewolf
  • Strong connection with nature and wildlife
