Parmanu

ParmanU is a high-tech superhero created by Raj Comics. Equipped with a powerful atomic suit, he can fly, fire energy blasts, and use advanced weapons. He fights crime and protects the nation using technology, courage, and scientific power.

Powers & Abilities

  • Powered atomic armor suit
  • Flight capability
  • Energy and laser attacks
  • Advanced weapons and gadgets
  • Enhanced strength and durability
