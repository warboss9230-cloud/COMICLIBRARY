Bhokal

Bhokal is a legendary warrior prince from the magical kingdom of Parilok. Blessed with immense strength, divine weapons, and royal courage, he fights dark forces, demons, and evil rulers to protect justice and honor.

Powers & Abilities

  • Superhuman strength and endurance
  • Master swordsman and warrior
  • Wields magical weapons
  • Royal leadership and battle strategy
  • Enhanced combat instincts
