Nagraj

Nagraj is one of the most iconic superheroes from Raj Comics. He is known as the "King of Snakes" and has the ability to control millions of snakes. Nagraj fights against evil, using his strength, intelligence, and his deadly weapon, the "Nagpasha."

Powers & Abilities

  • Control over millions of snakes
  • Deadly poison attacks
  • Superhuman strength and speed
  • High-level intelligence and strategy
  • Ability to regenerate and heal
